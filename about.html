Our Profile

Elite Healthcare Institution

The Avaneer Medical Center is an elite healthcare institution committed to provide state-of-the-art, patient-centred care that is on par with the best treatments offered globally and aids in producing exceptional outcomes.

Every aspect of Avaneer Medical Center design is ergonomic. Our highest concerns are the comfort and satisfaction of our patients. To nurture a pleasant environment conducive to healing, we provide well equipped rooms with ample interiors and cutting edge facilities accompanying a calm and serene atmosphere. A team of multidisciplinary doctors with extensive local and international knowledge supports all of these facilities.
